Don't Wait Until January To Start Planning Your Trip

Yes, we touched on this in the tip above, but it's so important that it bears repeating.
Waiting to make reservations usually isn't a good idea if you have definitely decided to enjoy Mardi Gras in New Orleans.
You might get lucky, but there is no guarantee.
If you like to gamble and don't want to reserve the minimum night stay required at most hotels,
then you can wait and see if those hotels will relax their minimum night stay requirements if they have rooms that need to be filled.
But there is no guarantee this will happen.
You definitely don't want to come to New Orleans looking for a room.
